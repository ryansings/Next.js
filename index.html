What is Signal?

Signal is a framework for noticing the impulses and urges before they take over. It’s a treasure map of your own nervous system, designed for those who want to move fast, feel more, and act intentionally. No scripts. Just signals.

How It Works

Notice

Learn to catch the first flicker of tension, thought, or urge before it becomes a collapse. Your body talks first—listen.

Name

Identify the impulse and the old belief it’s tied to. Separating “you” from your default programming creates space for choice.

Navigate

Take action aligned with what actually matters to you, rather than reacting to old patterns. Small wins compound into real change.

Reflect

End the day with a simple check-in: what signals showed up, what did you notice, and what worked? Awareness grows with consistency.
